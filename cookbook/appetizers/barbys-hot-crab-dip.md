# Barby's Hot Crab Dip

!!! Original "A _Go Cook!_ print original."

<div class="recipe-meta">
<span><strong>Prep:</strong> 10 mins</span>
<span><strong>Cook:</strong> 10 mins</span>
<span><strong>Yields:</strong> 4-6 servings</span>
</div>

I always loved this dip which would come out at parties. Serve with your favorite crackers.

## Ingredients

- [x] 8 ounces cream cheese
- [x] 1 can of king crab, drained
- [x] &frac12; teaspoon prepared horseradish
- [x] 2 tablespoons onion, minced
- [x] 2 tablespoons milk
- [x] salt and pepper to taste
- [x] &frac12; c almonds, toasted

## Instructions

- Preheat the oven to 375Â°F
- Combine all ingredients except almonds and place in a casserole dish.
- Top with almonds.
- Cover and bake for 20 minutes.
- Serve hot, with crackers for dipping and spreading.

## Chef's Notes

!!! note "Source"

I think Barby was a college friend of Mom's.

!!! note "My Take"

You can take the flavor and kick of this tasty dip up a notch by doubling or tripling the horseradish,
onion, and using fresh cracked black pepper. Shake in some crushed red pepper flakes and a little Old Bay
for some extra heat.
