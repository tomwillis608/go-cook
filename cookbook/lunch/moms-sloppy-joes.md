## Ingredients

- [x] 1 &frac12; pounds ground beef
- [x] 1 onion, chopped
- [x] &frac12; c ketchup
- [x] tablespoon yellow mustard
- [x] can chicken gumbo soup
- [x] cup water
- [x] 3-ounce can mushroom bits and pieces, drained
- [x] salt and pepper
- [x] optional, 6 sandwich rolls, toasted

## Instructions

- Cook the beef and onions together until lightly browned, breaking up the meat.
- Add the remaining ingredients, except rolls.
- Cover, bring to a boil, drop temperature to simmer about 30 minutes.
- Serve on toasted rolls or other bread.
